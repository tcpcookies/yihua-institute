Traditional Shoe Machinery

A complete range of conventional shoe-making equipment for comprehensive production solutions

Beyond our intelligent automation solutions, iHua Institute provides a full spectrum of traditional shoe machinery — from assembly lines and cutting machines to sewing and auxiliary equipment — ensuring we can meet the complete equipment needs of any footwear factory, whether establishing a new facility or upgrading an existing one.

🏭

Shoe Assembly Lines

Complete conveyor-based shoe assembly lines for various footwear types. Customizable length, speed, and workstation configuration to match your specific production workflow and capacity requirements.

✂️

Cutting Machines

Hydraulic and mechanical cutting presses for leather, synthetic, and textile materials. Various tonnage options available. Includes automatic feeding systems and die-cutting solutions.

🪡

Sewing Machines

Industrial sewing machines for shoe upper stitching, including flat-bed, post-bed, and cylinder-bed configurations. Computer-controlled stitching patterns for consistent quality.

🔥

Lasting Machines

Toe lasting, heel lasting, and side lasting machines. Hot and cold systems available. Precision lasting for consistent shoe shape and quality across all pairs.

🔨

Sole Attaching Machines

Hydraulic sole pressing and attaching equipment. Various pressure and platen sizes available. Used with heat-activated or cold cement processes.

🌡️

Heating & Drying Equipment

Industrial ovens, heat-setting machines, and drying tunnels for adhesive activation, material shaping, and moisture removal. Temperature-controlled for process consistency.

✨

Finishing Machines

Polishing, cleaning, ironing, and quality inspection stations. Final touch equipment ensuring every pair meets quality standards before packaging.

📦

Auxiliary Equipment

Supporting equipment including conveyors, material handling systems, air compressors, vacuum systems, and environmental control units for complete factory infrastructure.
