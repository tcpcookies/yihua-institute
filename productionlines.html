Intelligent Automated Production Lines

Complete smart production lines for diverse footwear categories — from sports to leather to boots

Each production line is designed with enclosed robot workstations, 3D vision scanning systems, and oil mist purification — delivering precision spraying, reduced chemical exposure, and eco-friendly manufacturing. Lines can be customized to your specific shoe type, capacity requirements, and factory layout.

01

Smart Shoe Production Line

Versatile intelligent production line configurable for casual leather shoes (circular type) and casual sports shoes (U-type). Comprehensive line with synchronized upper and outsole processing, 3D vision scanning, and enclosed robot workstations.

  • Types: Circular (leather) & U-type (sports)
  • Vision: 3D scanning system
  • Processing: Robot spraying & roughing
  • Enclosure: Full with purification
Line TypesCasual leather circular / Casual sports U-type
FeaturesRobot upper + outsole processing, 3D vision, enclosed workstations
02

Leather Shoe Intelligent Molding Production Line

Primarily for leather shoes. Outsole line uses 3D vision scanning to auto-generate robot trajectories for chemical treatment and glue spraying. Upper line features robot polishing, roughing, and spraying. Upper spray workstation shares trajectory data from outsole 3D vision scan. Fully enclosed with oil mist purification.

  • 3D vision auto-scanning & trajectory
  • Synchronized upper + outsole spraying
  • Full enclosure with purification
  • Eco-friendly & safe operation
ApplicationPrimarily leather shoes
PrecisionUpper & outsole spray ±1mm
Capacity100-120 pairs/hour
DimensionsL47m × W5.7m × H2m
Workers6 workers
PowerAC 220-380V, 50/60Hz, total 235KW
