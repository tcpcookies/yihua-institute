Who We Are

Guangdong Yihua Footwear Technology Research Institute Co., Ltd.

Guangdong Yihua Footwear Technology Research Institute Co., Ltd. is a high-tech enterprise integrating scientific R&D, manufacturing, and service — dedicated to providing comprehensive intelligent manufacturing solutions for the footwear industry. Founded in 2002, with over two decades of relentless pursuit, we have been at the forefront of footwear intelligence, automation, and digitalization.

Our team brings together industry veterans with decades of footwear manufacturing expertise, seasoned engineers, and PhD consultants. We are powered by a dynamic group of post-85s and post-90s researchers with Master's, Bachelor's degrees, and overseas study backgrounds. We maintain deep collaborations with multiple domestic universities on funding, technology, and project initiatives.

iHua Institute holds numerous invention and utility patents, and co-develops industrial robots, control systems, and vision technologies with Fortune 500 companies — specifically tailored for footwear manufacturing applications.

Why Choose Us

Proven Expertise & Capability

After more than a decade of development, manufacturing, and installation, iHua has become one of China's top mature automated footwear equipment suppliers.

Intelligent Automated Workstations 88%
Intelligent Automated Molding Production Lines 95%
Smart Factory Automation & Digitalization 82%

We have successfully installed over 20 intelligent production lines covering diverse footwear categories: sports shoes, flip-flop shoes, women's shoes, military boots, vulcanized shoes, special work shoes, safety shoes, motorcycle boots, and 3D-printed shoes. Our robotic solutions span grinding, polishing, spraying, chemical treatment, and painting — offering the optimal model for replacing manual labor with intelligent machines.

In 2025, we defined new standards with breakthrough technologies: 5D Spray Weaving, 6D Sole Printing, and Stereo Digital Precision Printing.
